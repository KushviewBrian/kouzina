Our Kitchen, Your Table

"Kouzina" means kitchen in Greek—a warm place where family and friends gather. We built The Kouzina around that idea: simple, colorful food made with care.

From whipped feta to bright, herb-packed sauces, we lean on quality ingredients and from-scratch prep. Pull up a chair at our custom live-edge wood bar, or grab a cozy corner booth.
